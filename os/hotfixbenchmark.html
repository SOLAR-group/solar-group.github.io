About

A hot fix is an unplanned improvements to specific time-critical issues deployed to a software system in production. There are currently no open-source benchmarks of hot fix examples that can be used to advance research on critical bug handeling. As such, we present HotBugs.jar, a benchmark of hot fixes deployed in Apache projects.
