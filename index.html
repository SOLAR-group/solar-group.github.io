Software Optimisation, Learning and Analytics Research

SOLAR is a group of researchers working on software engineering problems at the interface of software optimisation, machine/evolutionary learning and analytics.
The group currently consists of 13 members and is led by Prof Federica Sarro at University College London (UCL).
In SOLAR we promote and embrace open science, and therefore we strive to share our publications, data, tools, and models inside and outside the group, whenever possible.
